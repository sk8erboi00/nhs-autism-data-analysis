Data Skills Bootcamp – Portfolio Summary

Overview
This bootcamp provided end-to-end training across data analysis, business intelligence, databases, cloud computing, and Python analytics.
Through hands-on projects and structured exercises, I developed the ability to collect, transform, analyse, and present data to support operational and strategic decision-making in a professional environment.

Core Skills Developed
Data Analysis & Preparation: Excel, SQL, structured data cleaning, validation, and governance
Business Intelligence & Visualisation: Tableau, Power BI, dashboard design, trend analysis, storytelling
Databases & SQL: Relational modelling, schema design, joins, targeted querying
Cloud Computing (Azure): Data ingestion, transformation, storage, analytics services, compliance-aware architecture
Python for Analytics: Pandas, data exploration, aggregation, visualisation, reproducible workflows

Weekly Project Highlights
Week 1 – Data & Excel Fundamentals
Built a strong foundation in data preparation, transformation, and interpretation.
Focused on accuracy, governance, and communicating clear, evidence-based insights to stakeholders.
Analysed revenue and profit trends, identifying consistent long-term business growth despite short-term fluctuations.

Week 2 – Visual Analytics (Tableau & Power BI)
Designed dashboards to convert raw data into actionable insights.
Applied trend analysis, moving averages, and comparative visuals to assess product performance and target vs actual sales.
Identified that a small number of products generated the majority of revenue and volume.

Week 3 – Databases & SQL
Designed and implemented a relational database for a retail scenario.
Applied primary/foreign keys, joins, and structured queries to extract business insights.
Demonstrated end-to-end data flow from suppliers and inventory through to sales and customers.

Week 5 – Microsoft Azure Fundamentals
Explored cloud service models, deployment options, and regulatory considerations (GDPR, DPA 2018).
Worked with Azure data services to ingest, store, and analyse data.
Translated business requirements into scalable, secure cloud architectures using a case-study approach.

Week 6 – Python for Data Analysis
Used Pandas to load, clean, analyse, and visualise real-world datasets.
Automated analysis steps and produced reproducible outputs.
Built analytical dashboards to evaluate performance distributions, pass rates, and group-level differences.

Key Outcomes
Ability to deliver high-quality, insight-driven analysis
Strong foundation in BI dashboards and visual storytelling
Practical experience with SQL, relational databases, and cloud platforms
Confident use of Python for structured, reproducible analytics
Clear understanding of how data supports business performance and decision-making

Conclusion
This bootcamp significantly strengthened my technical capability, analytical confidence, and strategic understanding of data-driven organisations.
I am well prepared to contribute as a data analyst by designing scalable solutions, producing reliable insights, and supporting both operational and strategic objectives.
